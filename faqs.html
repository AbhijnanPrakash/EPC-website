Products

What UPS capacities do you supply?
From 1 kVA standalone units to 4000 kVA configurations, with system solutions spanning 350 VA to 4.8 MW depending on the application.
Which battery brands do you work with?
Panasonic, EnerRocket, Exide, Quanta and other proven manufacturers, in both lead-acid and Li-Ion chemistries from 7 AH to 400 AH.
Are you an authorized partner or a reseller?
Eternal Power is an authorized sales partner for major UPS OEMs, which means OEM-backed products, genuine spares and trained engineers.

Services & AMC

Do you service UPS systems you didn't sell?
Yes. Service support and AMCs are available for any make of UPS system.
What does an AMC cover?
Scheduled preventive maintenance, breakdown response and spares planning. Comprehensive contracts include spares; non-comprehensive contracts bill spares as used. Coverage can extend to BMS, fire alarm, panels, CCTV and access control.
Do you offer emergency support?
Yes. Round-the-clock support is available for critical equipment under AMC, and rental UPS/battery banks cover emergencies while repairs happen.

Installation

Do you handle installation and commissioning?
Yes. Certified engineers handle survey, installation, cabling, commissioning and calibration, with handover documentation.
Can you move our equipment to a new site?
Yes. Recommissioning and reinstallation for office or plant relocations are standard services.

Batteries & Testing

What is IR testing and why does it matter?
Internal resistance testing, done with HIOKI meters, measures each battery block's real condition. Rising IR reveals weak cells before they fail during an actual outage.
Do you buy back old equipment?
Yes. We buy back UPS systems of all types, batteries, IT assets and workstation equipment, with a documented process.
