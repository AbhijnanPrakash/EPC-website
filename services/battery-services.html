Battery Testing & Replacement

HIOKI IR testing for SMF battery banks, health reports and evidence-based replacement.

SERVESData centers, Healthcare, Banking & offices, Industrial

Overview

Batteries fail quietly and take your backup with them. Our IR (internal resistance) testing service uses HIOKI meters to measure the real condition of each block in an SMF battery bank, producing a health report that tells you which cells are weak, which bank needs replacement, and which is fine — so replacement decisions are made on evidence.

What's included

  • Internal resistance testing of SMF batteries with HIOKI meters
  • Block-by-block health report for the bank
  • Replacement recommendations with brand and sizing options
  • Supply and installation of replacement banks
  • Buyback and removal of the old batteries

Benefits

Evidence over guesswork

IR data shows actual battery condition, not the calendar age.

Avoid silent failure

Weak cells are found before a mains failure finds them for you.

Complete replacement service

Testing, supply, installation and old-bank buyback in one engagement.

How it works

01

Test

IR measurement of every block in the bank with a HIOKI meter.

 02

 03

Replace

Where needed, we supply and install the new bank and buy back the old one.

FAQ

Frequently asked

How often should battery banks be IR tested?
For critical loads, annually at minimum — ideally alongside scheduled AMC visits so trends are tracked over time.
Does testing interrupt our power protection?
IR testing is designed to be non-disruptive to the connected load. We schedule it with your team and take standard precautions.
