Overview

The battery is the part of a UPS that actually carries your load when the mains fails, and it is the part that degrades first. Eternal Power supplies batteries from 7 AH to 400 AH across lead-acid (SMF/VRLA) and Li-Ion chemistries, from brands including Panasonic, Exide, Quanta and EnerRocket, matched to the autonomy your application needs.

FAQ

Frequently asked

How often should UPS batteries be replaced?
Typical VRLA banks last three to five years depending on temperature, cycling and charging discipline. IR testing tells you the real condition, so you replace on evidence rather than on a calendar guess.
Lead-acid or Li-Ion , which should I choose?
Li-Ion offers longer life, smaller footprint and faster recharge at a higher upfront cost. Lead-acid remains economical for standard backup duties. We recommend based on your runtime and total cost of ownership.
Do you take away the old batteries?
Yes. We buy back old batteries and UPS systems as part of a replacement, with documented handover.
