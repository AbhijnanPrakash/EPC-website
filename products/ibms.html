Building Management Systems

Integrated BMS controlling HVAC, chillers, lighting and power, extendable to CCTV, access, fire and PA.

SPECHVAC · Chillers · LightingSPECCCTV · Access · FireSPECOne integrated platform

Overview

A Building Management System is the combination of hardware and software that controls, monitors and schedules a building's mechanical and electrical systems: HVAC, chillers, lighting and power for the workspace and the data center. Integrate CCTV, access control, fire alarm and suppression, public address, VFDs and motor starter panels into the same platform and you have an IBMS, one system, one picture of the building.

Applications

  • Corporate offices and commercial buildings
  • Data centers and technical facilities
  • Hospitals and healthcare campuses
  • Educational and institutional campuses

Features

Mechanical and electrical control

HVAC, chiller, lighting and power systems scheduled and controlled from one platform.

Security and safety integration

CCTV, access control, fire alarm, fire suppression and PA brought into the same view.

Drives and starters

VFDs and motor starter panels integrated for complete plant visibility.

Comfort with efficiency

Maintain workspace conditions while scheduling equipment to cut energy waste.

FAQ

Frequently asked

Can an IBMS be added to an existing building?
Yes. Existing HVAC, electrical and safety systems can usually be integrated in phases, starting with the systems that cost you the most to run or monitor manually.
Who operates the BMS after handover?
Your facility team can, or we can place trained technical manpower on site, or monitor it remotely from our command center, whichever fits your operation.
